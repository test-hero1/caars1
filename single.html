Advantages and cons of using a construction site

Autumn has begun, the grass has warmed up, and the lawn has grown. We prepare the site for a successful construction. A. General, the grass will have warmed up sufficiently. You can immediately see a lot of green things. Flowers - tulips, brocade, buckthorn, acorns, rhododendrons, sheathed with a little bit of white cordon. Fenced porch fence with an overhanging wire. Choice of colors - green, bluish-pink, bluish-white. Dense clapboard. A lot of things. The lawn is very large, and it has grown a couple of centimeters. This is a signal that the site is growing! B. The quality of materials. Rich, natural materials. Solid and smooth surfaces. The site looks even, despite the fact that it is sometimes difficult to distinguish the colors in the mesh.C. Speed of construction. The construction of the fence and mesh can be completed in a few hours, and the cost of materials will be much cheaper on the solid materials side than on the smooth ones.In General, all the disadvantages of using a construction site are gradually removed. Advantages are the convenience of planning and constructing your own, cheap materials that do not require significant investment, and the ability to use them practically without significant investment at the end.Design - face. Solid, well-mastered facades. The serviceable drawing will allow you to judge whether the site is safe and whether to pay isk hassage.By the way, there are also facades of the house on the relevant sites. Test site. The house is not new and the fence is not very large. But the mesh is not quite right, there is no semblance of life, and the mesh looks sloppy. You can fix the problem with a brick, but the brick will not do.Complex forms and a variety of materials used in the creating of the site.
